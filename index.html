Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you take an admission decision.

Are online degrees valid?

Validity depends on the university, program, intake and applicable recognition requirements. Always verify official information before enrolling.

Can working professionals pursue these programs?

Yes. Most online and executive programs are designed around flexible learning for working professionals.

How should I compare universities?

Compare recognition, curriculum, total fees, examination format, student support and suitability for your goal.
