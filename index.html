Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you take an admission decision.

Are online degrees valid?

Validity depends on the university, program, academic intake and applicable recognition requirements. Always verify current official information before enrolling.

Can working professionals pursue these programs?

Yes. Most online and executive programs are designed around flexible learning for working professionals.

How should I compare universities?

Compare recognition, curriculum, total fees, examination format, learning support and suitability for your career goal.
