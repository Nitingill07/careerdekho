Is an Online Degree Valid in India?

What learners should verify before taking admission.

The validity of an online degree depends on factors such as the university, the program, the academic session and applicable recognition requirements. Learners should verify official information before paying fees.

What to check

  • The university's recognition status
  • Whether the specific program is offered in online mode
  • The applicable academic session
  • Examination and academic requirements
  • How the qualification fits your career or further-study plans

Do not rely only on verbal claims. Always review official documentation and the latest information relevant to your intake.
